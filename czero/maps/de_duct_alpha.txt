de_duct_alpha - Bomb/Defuse
an Original map by Blues

The Bilderberg Group gained plenty of wealth throughout the past years but plenty is not enough for the richest people in the world. 
Looking at the succes of SARS-COV 2 they have developed a new even stronger strain and a new vaccine against it. 

Few people knows what is really going on but a group of construction workers has figured out that the virus is to be spread through the drinking water.


Terrorists:
Plant a bomb close to the SARS-COV-X virus or the vaccines box.

Counter-Terrorists:
Prevent the terrorist from destroying the virus or the vaccines
